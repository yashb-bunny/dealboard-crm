✦ Features

Everything your sales team needs

Built for speed. Designed for results. Dealboard gives your team the tools to close more deals with less effort.

📈
Visual Sales Pipeline
Drag-and-drop Kanban board across 8 deal stages. See your entire pipeline at a glance and move deals forward instantly.
🤖
AI-Powered Assistant
Chat naturally with your CRM via Telegram. Add contacts, create deals, and get summaries — all through conversation.
📊
Real-time Analytics
Live dashboards showing win rates, pipeline value, and revenue forecasts. Make data-driven decisions every day.
🏢
Organisation Tracking
Link contacts to companies. Track all deals, notes, and activities per account. Never lose context again.
📥
Bulk CSV Import
Upload thousands of contacts, deals, and organisations in seconds. Smart mapping auto-links your data correctly.
🔔
Daily EOD Summaries
Automatic end-of-day report sent to your Telegram every evening. Stay informed even when you're away from your desk.
